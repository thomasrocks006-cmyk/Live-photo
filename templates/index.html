How to use on iPhone

  1. Convert your photo and download the ZIP file.
  2. Unzip — you'll get a .jpg and a .mov with the same name.
  3. AirDrop both files to your iPhone (select both before sharing).
  4. iOS saves them to your Camera Roll as a Live Photo automatically.
  5. Open iMessage, tap the photo icon, and select your Live Photo — it sends as a Live Photo! 🎉
